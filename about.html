About Spade

When Mr. Abhishek Kumar Jha and Ms. Pooja decided to start something new and opening up a Student's Organization seemed to be the best initation. It started with just a few members who were willing to dedicate themselves for a new Organization. And that was all our founder and co-founder needed- dedication! Having the motto of 'dig out the hidden talent', SPADE started conducting events ranging from sports to cultural across every field throughout the year in the University, each of them being successful ones! As the family started to grow, it promised to search and shove out the hidden talents and show them to the world enhancing confidence and self belief among all its members. After four years of its commencement, SPADE launched a new company with the name of SPADE EMS across the country which contains various features including internships in several departments for the students. It also featured in the Top 75 Startups of India! The hard-work, dedication and passionate behaviour of all the students towards SPADE is the reason of its major success!
